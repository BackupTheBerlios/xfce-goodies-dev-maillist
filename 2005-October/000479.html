Ok, it seems that we like dokuwiki now (see thunar.xfce.org/wiki and

propose to host on the new Xfce server with xfce-goodies.xfce.org domain:

Feedback section.

code: http://xfce.loculus.nl/files/xfce-goodies-wiki.tar.gz
