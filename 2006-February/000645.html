For now, pages are just plain html (I have no time to make something better...)
Since you add a plugin, 2 things can be done:
-Post a News
-Update the page who describe all the plugins

To have an access to these pages, you can use ssh to log in using your belios 's account. The server is shell.berlios.de
You can also use scp to copy these page to you pc, change them and then upload them in berlios.
The commands are:
To copy from Berloios to your pc:
scp username at shell.berlios.de:/home/groups/xfce-goodies/htdocs/ /home/Your_directory/name_of_the_file
To copy from your pc to Berloios:
scp /home/Your_directory/name_of_the_file username at shell.berlios.de:/home/groups/xfce-goodies/htdocs/

The files are:
contact.html    goodies.html  menu.html   
download.html   news.html     packages.html
index.html      top.html        

In your case, you just have to edit the news.html and packages.html pages.

Generally, it's me who maintain these pages, so if you want I can add your plugin myself. But if you to post the news and add the descrption yourself, you are free to do that.

Also, if you have any question... just ask ;-)
