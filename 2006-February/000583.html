Hey everyone,

There's something I've been laying on my hd for the past few months,
and now thought I might as well share it.

It's a panel plugin that listens dbus messages and displays them in the
panel and/or a popup window in the corner of the screen. It can be used
for example to display log event notifications (see scripts dir in
source tarball).

Regardless of it's name (which sucks btw, but I couldn't come up with a
better one), it has nothing to with the IM system originating from
Redmond, WA.

Screenie:
http://medusa.tutka.fi/~pasi/xfce4-messenger-screenshot.jpg

Tarball:
http://medusa.tutka.fi/~pasi/xfce4-messenger-plugin-0.1.0.tar.bz2

It requires the SVN panel and DBus >= 0.31. All feedback appreciated.

-Pasi
