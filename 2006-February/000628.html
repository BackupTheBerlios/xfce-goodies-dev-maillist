charge.

and then displays numbers under 100% (normal).

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Enable the battery plugin.
2. Connect the AC power.
3. The battery charge starts and the plugin displays status over 100%.

Actual Results:  
The battery plugin displays incorrect battery information.

Expected Results:  
Display the correct battery status.
