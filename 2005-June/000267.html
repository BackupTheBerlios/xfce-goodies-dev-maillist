I've seen different behavior of battpanel on two different laptops.

  /proc/acpi/thermal_zone/THRM/temperature
while on the 2nd laptop it has to be
  /proc/acpi/thermal_zone/ATF0/temperature

By looking at the source I saw that it also checks for the fan.
