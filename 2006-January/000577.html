You can have a look here:
https://developer.berlios.de/docman/display_doc.php?docid=394&group_id=2#newssh

Basically, it is creating a new directory called xfce4-xfapplet-plugin/ under the xfce-goodies one, just as you said.

Also you must be able to release a tarball and post them in the download section. For that, get logged into berlios and go to the xfce-goodies
page. On the left, You have something like this :

Project: Goodies for the Xfce desktop
Résumé du Projet
Forums de Discussion
Submit Bugs
Request Support
Request Features

Admin. du Projet


Go to the "Admin. du Projet" part. Then on the top of the  page, you have the "Edit/Release Files" menu. Go there and then you can click on quick-release a file and follow the instruction. Just know that you will also have to upload your tarball in the berlios ftp at:
ftp://ftp.berlios.de/incoming/.
