I imported the plugin to Xfce Goodies SVN, uploaded a tarball and
created a website for the thunar-archive-plugin 0.1.0, which I posted

http://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=910&release_id=9574

http://xfce.org/~benny/projects/thunar-archive-plugin/index.html

required cmdline switches.
